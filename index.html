Hi, I'm Jason.
I'm a full stack web developer.
Most recently, I am a graduate of General Assembly's Web Developer Immersive course, but my interest in tech goes back to my childhood. We had this game called Reader Rabbit on our computer, and since it was educational, I was allowed to play it even when I was grounded. I taught myself to type when I was grounded. That's why I could type at 60wpm when I was 9. I wasn't grounded that often, I just made good use of my time.

My interest in programming goes back to middle school when my friend taught me how to "hack" a game called Diablo. Really we were just loading hacks that others had written, but I felt cool. That's why, in high school, I took both C++ and Visual Basic, which was every class they had at the time.
